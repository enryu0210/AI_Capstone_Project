This dataset has a simple structure. It contains three sub-datasets, where Dataset_Prostate_part1 and Dataset_Prostate_part2 are from in-house da-Vinci prostatactomy videos, and Dataset_Cholec80 is created from the publically available cholec80 video dataset. Within each sub-dataset, the smoky image is named as *_img.png and its corresponding smoke free ground truth is named as *_gt.png .
